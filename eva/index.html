    CONCEPT + ILLUSTRATION + DESIGN

  • A fun take on the simple This VS That game for kids and up. Inanimate objects are brought to life as they battle each other, and the user simply selects the winner and gets added to the collection.

  • 100 objects were digitally drawn for phase 1, with plans to develop into more characters, integration into another game, and collaboration with other artists.

  • Nifty little feature: the user can also continue to play on their notification screen. Launching soon, stay tuned.
