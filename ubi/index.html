• UBIWORLD: RAVING RABBIDS + PETZ FAMILY
    2011

    ILLUSTRATION + ANIMATION

  • New islands were to be added onto tweens' online virtual world Ubiworld - Raving Rabbids and Petz Fantasy.

  • Illustrated from the team artist's sketch and animated both Raving Rabbids and Petz Fantasy islands, where users can roam around and interact with elements within the game for a little bit of delight.

  • A great animation exercise as these are existing characters/designs that have strict guidelines, but the team was given freedom to explore creatively. Via Soap Creative.
