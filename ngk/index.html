• GEOGAMES + STUMP YOUR PARENTS
    2008 + 2010

    ILLUSTRATION + ANIMATION + DEV

  • Series of flash games designed and developed for National Geographic Kids. Via Soap Creative.

  • Stump Your Parents quiz game was to be designed and look like a Japanese game show, the result is a blocky high-contrast design to balance the text-heavy game. Custom-designed font was also created to match the art direction.

  • With the 2008 Summer Olympics mania, the team designed and developed a set of 5 sporty games called GeoGames. Users were also allowed to post their scores and compete with friends.
