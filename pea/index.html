• PEA-MAN
    2012 - 2013

    ANIMATION + ILLUSTRATION + DIRECTION

  • He is a pea, and he's all about simplicity. Peaman was born from a scanned chinagraph drawing and a blot of green water-colour.

  • Pea-man was seen on numerous tv commercials as a "deliverer of specials" for supermarket giant Woolworths.

  • The 15' and 30' ads were animated in Flash and the character has transitioned from tweening rigged body parts, to frame by frame drawing with a custom brush stroke applied for his outlines. This preserves the drawn look he has had throughout. Toon Boom Harmony was also experimented with to attempt a quicker approach to achieve the right look of the line stroke.

  • Having to produce time-sensitive multiple ads was a definite challenge, but what is kept in mind is to dish out a charmingly simple animation that does its purpose - deliver the message. Via Heckler.
