• INTERACTIVE STORY MOBILE APP
    2014 - 2016

    CREATIVE DIRECTION + DESIGN + ILLUSTRATION

  • An in-depth interactive story mobile app based on a written manuscript about Singapore’s Reserves.

  • The story’s richness needed to be represented in a fresh and modern way, with highly customized illustrations that were digitally hand-drawn, making the story personal and compelling.

  • The use of iconography was essential to balance the text-heavy nature of the app.

  • An internal version with a chat function for GICians to communicate was launched first. For the full public version, rich archival resources were showcased, and a companion site was also built. App is no longer available. Via VML.
