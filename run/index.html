• ANIMATED SERIES FOR KIDS
    2011

    CREATOR + DIRECTION + ANIMATION + MUSIC

  • Winning entry for international animation competition held by GRAPHIC 2011 Festival @ Sydney Opera House. Collaborated work as ZZZ Collective.

  • Different elements added every round during the competition, making the process exciting and unpredictable. The concept of Run Amok! evolved naturally through creative teamwork.

  • An original music composition was created to suit the high-impact visuals of the concept.

  • Run Amok! was fully animated in Flash and composited in After Effects.
