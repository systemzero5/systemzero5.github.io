• KFC GAMES
    2009 + 2010

    ILLUSTRATION + ANIMATION

  • For 2 years in a row, CDROM games were designed, illustrated and animated for KFC as a gift with every meal purchase for kids.

  • Focus groups were done with kids aged 5 to 11, themes and types of games were selected based on the research. The games were also chosen carefully to appeal to both girls and boys.

  • A generic character was designed to have interchangeable outfits and appearances, while keeping the main boy and girl characters really simple.

  • Paper craft models were also custom designed to represent a character for each theme, as a bonus download.

  • For the second year, design and development amendments were made to the game shell to make it more unified and easier for future updates.

  • An excellent design, illustration and animation process, worked very closely with team game developers to maintain consistency and making sure the games were absolutely fun for kids. Via Soap Creative.
