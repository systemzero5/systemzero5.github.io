• RAVING RABBIDS: TRAVEL IN TIME
    2011

    ILLUSTRATION + ANIMATION

  • Browser flash game designed and developed for Ubisoft's launch of Raving Rabbids Travel in Time for 3DS and Wii.

  • Characters were illustrated and animated from scratch based on the console game. Once the levels were created and tested using a robust custom level editor, game design, illustration and animation assets followed.

  • An enjoyable work process that produced a very entertaining award-winning game that won the Gold award for Best Use of Online Media at the MI6 Game Marketing Awards 2011. Via Soap Creative.
